Simple Shivratri Devotion

  1. Light a diya and incense with a pure intention.
  2. Offer milk, water, and bilva leaves to Shiva Lingam.
  3. Chant “Om Namah Shivaya” with focused breath.
  4. Spend a few silent minutes in gratitude and prayer.
